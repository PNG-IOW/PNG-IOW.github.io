The Needles

A world-famous landmark on the Isle of Wight, UK, made up of three chalk stacks that lead to the Needles Lighthouse.

"This place is a full day of amazing experiences."

- Steph

Osborne House

Former retreat for Queen Victoria and Prince Albert (circa 1845) still retains a large collection of the royal couple's possessions, art work and photographs.

"What an amazing place, full of history and lovely surroundings. You can tell that Osborne House was a special place for Queen Victoria and her family."

- Richard

Shanklin Beach

Scenic recreational beach offering cabanas & boat rentals, with a nearby esplanade of amusements.

"Beautiful beach great amusements amazing fish and chips and topped off with a mr whippy couldn't ask for more"
